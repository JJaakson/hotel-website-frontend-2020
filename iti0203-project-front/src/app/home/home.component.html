Welcome!

The 3-star superior Hotel Trivago is located in the center of Tartu, in the Emajõgi business district. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are the attractions of Tartu - Aura Water Center and Ahhaa Science Center.

Modern design, professional service and an attractive location make Trivago the most desired destination for both near and far guests.

Tartu's largest hotel with 205 rooms, a spacious a la carte restaurant and the largest conference center in Southern Estonia equipped with modern technology.

The Old Town, the Vanemuine Theater, the Concert Hall, University of Tartu and shopping centers are only a few steps away from the hotel. Many rooms offer views of the city and the Emajõgi River, the view is breathtaking.
