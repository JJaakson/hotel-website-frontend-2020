LOCATION

Hotel Trivago is located in the center of Tartu, in the Emajõgi business district. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are the attractions of Tartu - Aura Water Center and Ahhaa Science Center. The Old Town, the Vanemuine Theater, the Concert Hall, University of Tartu and shopping centers are only a few steps away from the hotel.
